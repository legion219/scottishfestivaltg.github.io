Do you have a Clan or Cultural group you would like to promote at the Penticton Scottish Festival?

Standard 10’x10' space is free for the day for clan booths that are for information only and have no retail activity. Tables and chairs will be provided by request only at a cost of $10.00 per table and $2.00 per chair. Extra 10’x10' space will be provided by request at a cost of $10.00. Tents will not be provided. Admission will be available at the gate the morning of the event.
