GETTING HERE
Parking is free at the Penticton Scottish Festival!
However, spaces in the Kings Park Parking Lot are limited. If you are not able to find parking in the parking lot, street parking is permitted on most streets around Kings Park.
Please be conscious of posted parking signs, respectful of people living in the neighborhood and do not block any driveways.

Spectators can enter from GATE 1 (shown with a Red Star) or  GATE 2 (Shown with a Purple Star.)
Competitors are asked to please enter the field from GATE 2.
